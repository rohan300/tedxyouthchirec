OUR THEME—ECHO

The theme for TEDxYouth@CHIREC 2019 is ECHO; Explore Create Honor Observe. Our stage will resonate the essence of these aspects in our lives: macro or micro in nature, the relevance prevails.

Explore

As individuals, it is essential that one explores new opportunities, new avenues, and new prospects constantly. It is with a certain degree of curiosity and accompanying will power to take up new activities that we progress in life. We can explore things that are familiar to us or completely new; at the end of the day, we walk away with more than we came with.

Create

In our world today, people are ever-changing the landscape of technology, food, tourism and every other industry there is. All of this change comes from one's spark to create something new for the world around them. When we make something, we quantify our passion and hard work with something that others can benefit from.

Honour

Any industry, any job, any profession, requires honor and respect for those around us. We develop a network of connections based on the way we approach and interact with people. To succeed and progress with one's passion, one would need to accept the place of everyone around them.

Observe

We're always told to learn from our mistakes; however, learning from others' mistakes will definitely prove to be productive. As we witness the progress in the field around us, we will either be spurred with motivation or we will learn about what not to do. The maximum learning takes place when one chooses to observe the dynamics around them with an open mind.

About Chirec

Established in 1989 by Ratna Reddy, CHIREC was founded as a vibrant summer camp for children to develop their skills in arts, sports, and technology. Over the last 30 years, CHIREC International School has grown to become a leader in K-12 education as an institution that instils a passion for learning, and innovation. CHIREC International School offers CBSE, IB and Cambridge curricula, and is recognized as one of the best schools in Hyderabad, Telangana and in India.
